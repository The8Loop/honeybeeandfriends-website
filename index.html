Our daycare's mission is to enhance creative growth though social interaction, art and play while sprinkling in academics to help little Honey Bees prepare for elementary.

State certified

We maintain compliance with all state regulations at all times. Your child's safety and comfort are our number one concern.

Experienced, certified care

With years of teaching experience as a mother and teacher, Susan helps guide, nurture and encourage your child to grow through creative play, teamwork, and independent learning.
About the Daycare
Ms. Susan earned a BA in Social Work from Texas A&M and a teaching certificate in EC-12 Special Needs. She also is CPR certified, and is an experienced CNA. She has worked in the school system and enjoys spending time with her own children.
Honeybee and Friends Daycare is dedicated to providing a fun, educational, creative, and safe learning environment. We provide nutritious meals and physical activities while encouraging kids to be team players as well as independent learners.
Honeybee and Friends Daycare meets the qualifications for a Listed Daycare. This includes FBI background checks, fingerprinting, and state training. We have completed sleep safe training, food handler certification, and CPR certification. You can be confident we provide a safe and stable environment for your child to grow.
